Big Services

We are a fleet is comprised of Tri-drive and Tandem Tractors equipped with fuel , crude oil and produced water pumping equipment along with a fleet of super bee trailers and wagons. Our fleet routinely delivers fuel / crude products to some of the most non accessible areas in Northern Alberta. Because of our commitment to excellence our dispatch is available 24-7 providing support for our customers and fleet. We have full service shops located in Whitecourt and Slave Lake Alberta providing in shop service and emergency roadside repairs.

Services

Fuel Delivery
For over 10 years we have provided short notice and regular contracted service to remote tanks, bulk and retail stations throughout Alberta , our fleet of tandem, tridem and super b trailers are ready and available to pickup at all major refineries in Alberta.
Crude Oil and Produced Water
Specializing in Tractor service for Heavy & Light Crude, Condensate and Produced Water Hauling.
Tractor Service
Provide tractor / Hotshot service on short notice throughout northern Alberta , vans , reefers , flats, tanks , haz-mat
Heavy Vehicle Serice and Repair
Repair facilities located in Slave Lake and Whitecourt Alberta,Certified vehicle inspections, Heavy Duty Repairs on Tractor,Truck,Trailers and commercial units. Evening and weekend emergency call outs .
Logistical Analysis and Planning
At Big Services we specialize in innovative logistic solutions that provide your company with a competitive advantage. From commercial to consumer products distribution, let us design a just-in-time supply chain that enhances the growth of your business. Are you trying to move full loads or even one pallet ? call us , we specialize in shipments to and from the USA , we can help arrange your border crossing ,warehousing if needed and hotshot service to make sure your product is delivered in record time.
Aviation Fuel
We offer logistics and sales for bulk and drummed aviation fuel to main and remote sites located in Northern Alberta.
Real Time Tracking
With the use of Qualicom satellite we can pin point your product while in transit.
